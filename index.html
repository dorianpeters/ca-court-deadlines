How to Use the Calculator

  1. Select a Starting Date: Click the date input box to bring up a calendar and pick your key event date.
  2. Choose Calculation Mode: Use the toggle switch to choose between 'Calendar Days' (includes all days and adjusts the final date) or 'Court Days' (skips weekends and holidays entirely).
  3. Calculate Custom Deadlines: For specific timeframes, enter numbers in the text box (e.g., -90, 60), then click 'Update Deadlines'.
